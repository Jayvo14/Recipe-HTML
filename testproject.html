Fried Chicken Wings

Enjoy classic fried chickens, perfect for any game day or get together.
Just follow these easy steps and finish them off by making your favorite sauce to dip them.



Prep Time:
1 hour 20 Minutes

Cook Time:
15 Minutes

Total Time:
1 hour 35 Minutes


Ingredients











Directions

  1. Rinse wings under cold water and thoroughly wash.
  2. Pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Refridgerate for one hour.
  4. Place the flour in a large bowl and season with the salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Place the chicken wings in the bowl; gently toss to coat with flour mixture.
  6. Heat 4 inches of oil in a large pot to 350 degress F.
  7. Add the chicken wings to the pot in batches of about 6-8 wings. Fry for 10-12 minutes or until wings are deep golden brown and cooked through. Repeat with remaining wings.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the butter and lemon pepper seasoning. Brush the butter mixture over the chicken wings and toss to coat. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
  9. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley sprigs if desired.
